About us

Cubeark is the creator of sustainable living spaces. Our design is beyond just creating a house, we design environments where you can have maximum life experience with your family, friends and yourself.

We believe today sustainability is very important in all fields, therefore we continuously strive to design our houses in a way that will be in harmony its environment and the nature at full extent.

Cubeark is ready to offer you its uniqely pre- designed type houses, which encompass the latest trends and ideas both in modern and traditional architecture, but we are also ready and dedicated to create the house of your dreams from scratch, through a very sophisticated and smooth design process!
